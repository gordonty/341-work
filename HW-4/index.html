Crescent Cinnamon Rolls


These are so good yummy and easy.


From thehungryhousewife.com, which has pictures for you as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 tube or 8 count Pillsbury Crescent Rolls, unrolled and separated.
Filling
  • 4 tbs butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspons cinnamon
Glaze
  • 2 tbs butter, melted
  • 1/4 cups and 2tbs powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    1. Preheat oven to 375o
    2. Place unrolled and separated crescent rolls on an un greased cookie sheet
    Filling
    1. in a small bowl mix together the butter, sugar, and cinnamon
    Assemble
    1. Evenly spread the cinnamon butter over the crescent rools and roll up tightly
    2. Place tip side down
    3. Bake for 10-12 minutes (at 375o)
    Glaze
    1. In small bowl mix together the butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth
    2. Place in zip lock bag and snip tiny corner off.
      • Using a spoon to drizzle the glass will work as well
    3. Drizzle the glaze over the cinnamon rolls

    Glossary


    Crescent
    A shape that resembles part of a ring coming to points at the end, like the moon in it's first quater

    Powdered Sugar
    A sugar product made of pulverized granulated sugar
